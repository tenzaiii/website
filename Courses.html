Courses We Offer

Marian School of Quezon City is a Filipino, Christian Institution of learning established by an academic community of committed and responsible individuals

STEM

The STEM strand, which stands for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, is an academic program designed to give students the fundamental knowledge and abilities in these areas.

HUMSS

A course of study intended to foster a profound comprehension of society, culture, human behavior, and the wider world.

ICT

A strand is a specialized academic program designed to get students ready for the quickly changing digital environment.

ABM

A strand is a specialized academic track that gives students a thorough understanding of business management, marketing, entrepreneurship, and finance concepts and practices.

CULINARY

A specialized academic track called the Culinary Arts strand aims to give students extensive training and expertise in the culinary industry.

Facilities

Facilities are the resources and physical framework that underpin the different operations, services, and activities that are provided by a company or institution. These consist of amenities, machinery, buildings, and other structures made to fulfill particular demands.

Library

In the context of education, the school library acts as a center for learning, investigation, and discovery. Students can access a variety of resources, such as books, digital materials, periodicals, and multimedia resources, in this dynamic space.

Computer Laboratory

A computer laboratory is a specialized space with computers and associated technology that is intended to facilitate a range of practical, educational, and research endeavors. It gives researchers and students access to the digital, hardware, and software resources they need for learning and experimentation in disciplines like multimedia design, engineering, computer science, and information technology.

Science Laboratory

A science laboratory is a specially designed area furnished with tools, supplies, and equipment for carrying out scientific research and experiments in a range of fields, including biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science. It gives students the chance to apply their theoretical knowledge in real-world situations and engages them in experiential learning.
